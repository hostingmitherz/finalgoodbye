Farewell and Thank You!

Dear Hosting mit Herz Community,

After a wonderful journey, it is with a heavy heart that we announce the closure of Hosting mit Herz. On October 12th, the servers will be permanently shut down.

We want to take this moment to sincerely thank each and every one of you for being a part of this project. Your support and involvement have meant the world to us.

A special thanks goes out to those of you who generously donated. Your contributions, though small in comparison to the challenges we faced, helped us keep this service running for as long as we could. We truly appreciate every bit of support you gave.

We also want to extend our gratitude to all of you for your patience over the years, especially during times of service interruptions and technical issues. Your understanding and kindness did not go unnoticed, and it allowed us to continually improve and offer the best service we could.

For those wishing to download their data, further instructions can be found on our Discord: https://hostingmitherz.de/discord.

Once again, thank you for the unforgettable moments and for being part of Hosting mit Herz.

With heartfelt appreciation,
The Hosting mit Herz Team
