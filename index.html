Farewell for Now

Dear Hosting mit Herz Community,

It is with a very heavy heart that we share the news that Hosting mit Herz was shut down on October 12th, at exactly 00:00. Watching the servers go dark was not easy; it was one of the hardest decisions we’ve ever had to make, and yes, there were certainly some tears as we said our goodbyes.

Over the years, Hosting mit Herz was more than just a hosting service – it was a community, a family. Each one of you brought life, warmth, and laughter to this project, and we are deeply grateful for all the unforgettable moments we shared together. Your support, patience, and understanding during our highs and lows made every challenge worthwhile, and it’s something we will always cherish.

To those who generously donated to help us stay afloat, your kindness did not go unnoticed. Although the hurdles we faced were significant, your contributions helped keep the heart of this project beating for as long as possible. We are truly humbled by your generosity.

Even though the servers are no longer online, we have securely saved all data. If you wish to retrieve your data, please kindly request it before October 31st (Halloween). We will do our best to respond promptly, but please bear with us as processing requests might take a few days. Instructions on how to request your data are available on our Discord: https://hostingmitherz.de/discord.

This goodbye may only be temporary. We are already dreaming of a possible return, stronger and more resilient than ever. So please, stay tuned, keep in touch, and don’t lose hope – we may yet surprise you with a comeback that brings Hosting mit Herz back to life.

Thank you, from the bottom of our hearts, for being part of this journey. For your patience, your kindness, and your unwavering support. As we step away for now, know that every one of you has left an indelible mark on our hearts. Until we meet again...

With love and deepest appreciation,
The Hosting mit Herz Team
