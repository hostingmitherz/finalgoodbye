Farewell for Now

Dear Hosting mit Herz Community,

With a heavy heart, we have to announce that Hosting mit Herz has been shut down as of October 12th, 00:00. The decision was not taken lightly and indeed, it was a tough one, bringing a few tears to our eyes as we watched the servers go offline.

We want to extend our deepest gratitude to all of you who have been a part of this journey. Your support, patience, and the moments we shared together have meant the world to us.

Although the servers are down, all data has been securely saved. If you wish to retrieve your data, you can kindly request it before October 31st (Halloween). Please note that processing requests may take a few days. You can reach out to us on Discord for further instructions: https://hostingmitherz.de/discord.

This isn’t necessarily the end. We are exploring the possibility of a return someday. So, stay tuned and keep an eye on future updates. We may come back stronger and with even more to offer.

Thank you once again for the unforgettable moments and for being part of Hosting mit Herz. We truly appreciate your understanding and the kindness you've shown throughout this journey.

With heartfelt appreciation,
The Hosting mit Herz Team
